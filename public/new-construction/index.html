New Construction Roofing FAQs

  • We work directly with your GC and trades to align underlayment, flashing, and shingle/metal install with framing and exterior milestones. You’ll get a clear start window and daily updates so inspections and other trades stay on track.

  • Architectural asphalt shingles or standing seam metal are our top picks. Both handle Southwest Michigan’s freeze–thaw cycles and wind well. We also install ice & water shield at eaves/valleys and proper ventilation to extend roof life.

  • Yes. We follow local code requirements (including ice barrier zones) and manufacturer specifications. We coordinate any required inspections and provide documentation for your closeout package.

  • Scope typically includes decking review, underlayment, flashing, ventilation, and installation of your selected shingle or metal system, plus jobsite protection and daily cleanup. Final inspection and warranty registration are included.
