Professional New Roof Installation in Southwest Michigan

1–3 Day Replacements. Clean Sites. Clear Communication.

We deliver full roof replacements with tear-off, decking repair as needed, and installation of architectural shingles or metal systems. Every install follows manufacturer specs and local code, with careful property protection and daily cleanup.

Expect a detailed estimate, dependable scheduling, and proactive updates. We’ll help you choose materials that balance curb appeal, performance, and budget—and deliver a roof built to last.



Our New Roof Process

  1. Inspection and written estimate
  2. Tear-off and decking repairs (as needed)
  3. Ice & water, underlayment, flashing, and ventilation
  4. Installation of your selected shingle or metal system
  5. Final walkthrough, cleanup, and warranty registration
