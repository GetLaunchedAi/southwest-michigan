About Us

Southwest Michigan Roofing & Construction

Founded in July 2023, Southwest Michigan Roofing & Construction is a family-rooted business built on decades of hands-on experience. Originally established in 2023 under a different name as a general construction company, we quickly evolved into a specialized provider of roofing, siding, and exterior construction services throughout Southwest Michigan.

Our team is made up of seasoned professionals with deep industry knowledge and a strong commitment to quality workmanship. While we've proudly served as subcontractors for leading contractors in the region, we’re now focused on building direct relationships with homeowners and property managers who value long-lasting, reliable construction solutions.

As a company, we're growing fast—with a dedicated team, strong work ethic, and the drive to become one of Michigan’s top roofing and exterior service providers. Our marketing and digital presence are being led by a team member currently studying Construction Management at Michigan State University, combining real-world experience with formal education to scale the business strategically.

Leopoldo Morales CEO-Founder
