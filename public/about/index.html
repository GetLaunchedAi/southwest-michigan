About Us

About Southwest Michigan Roofing & Construction

Southwest Michigan Roofing & Construction was officially founded in July 2023, but its roots go deeper. Originally launched under a different name as a general construction company, the business was started by a dedicated father-and-son team committed to honest work and reliable service. Over time, we shifted our focus toward what we do best—residential roofing, siding, and exterior construction throughout Kalamazoo, Portage, and surrounding Michigan communities.

Backed by a team of highly skilled professionals with years of industry experience, we’ve spent time working as trusted subcontractors for established contractors. Today, we're taking our own name to the forefront—building our brand, earning recognition, and bringing top-tier craftsmanship directly to homeowners and property managers across Southwest Michigan.

We're a locally owned and operated business driven by strong values, quality results, and long-term client relationships. Our marketing is led by a Michigan State University Construction Management student, blending hands-on construction knowledge with forward-thinking digital strategies. With a growing online presence and a crew that delivers on every promise, we’re confident in one thing—we’re just getting started.
